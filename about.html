About Me

Who am I?

I am a Swedish student studying IT, focusing on hardware like servers and networking, and software like arduino and web development. I am currently in my third year in gymnasiet,[1] and am planning on going to an yrkeshögskola[2] hoping to study DevOps for two years.

What do I do?

As mentioned I am currently studying full time. On my free time I like to code (see my projects here), learning new things, playing video games if I have the time, and photography (see the gallery and my Instagram).

Currently (until May 2024), I am the CEO of a company made through UF.[3] Together with some friends, we are currently developing and are going to sell a passive audio mixer that lets you combine different sources to one pair of headphones. Find us at hardbergsljud.se.
